Value Proposition

Kinderlech farms is poised to make fresh and healthy chicken meat available for restaurants, hotels and other bulk users with the convenience of having it delivered at your doorstep at a very competitive price. Our chicken meat is produced under the best hygienic conditions with no synthetic additives or hormones used. We also deliver our products 24-48 hours after processing in a bid to deliver them fresh to our users/consumers.

SWOT Analysis

The following SWOT analysis captures the key strengths and weaknesses of the business, and describes the opportunities and threats facing Kinderlech farms

Contact for More

Strengths

Kinderlech farms has strength in that it sells high quality poultry products delivered in unique variants using its unique specially mixed healthy spices.

Weaknesses

The major weakness Kinderlech farms might experience is that the products are new as our chicken variants were introduced into the market about 8 months ago. Our brand is gaining momentum but is still weak. With adequate marketing and superior customer service, this weakness can be effectively mitigated against.

Opportunities

The focus on delivering high quality is an opportunity for Kinderlech farms in that there is always a market for the very best. There is also opportunity within its reputation for delivering the best quality in a forthright manner

Threats

One of the biggest threats to our operations is the threat of disease outbreaks on poultry farms and biosecurity. This will be mitigated against using our training and experience acquired over the years and strict adherence to vaccination programs.
